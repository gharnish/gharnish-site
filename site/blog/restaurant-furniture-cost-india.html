What Does Restaurant Furniture Cost in India?

Furniture is one of the first big line items when you open a venue — and one of the easiest to misjudge. Here are real 2026 price ranges for chairs, tables and complete sets, plus a worked budget for a 40-seat café.

Gharnish·Price guide·6 min read

Chairs: where most of the budget goes

You buy more chairs than anything else, so the per-chair price drives your total. Commercial café and restaurant chairs start around ₹2,100 and rise with material, comfort level and finish — metal and moulded designs at the accessible end, upholstered and lounge seating higher up.

Tables: base + top

A restaurant table is priced in two parts — the base and the top — which is why two tables the same size can cost very differently.

Complete dining sets

If you'd rather price by the set, complete dining sets range from approximately ₹19,000 to ₹1,59,999 depending on tier, size and materials — entry-level café sets at the lower end, premium fine-dining and feature sets at the top.

A worked example: 40-seat café

A simple, realistic build using mostly 2-seaters with a few 4-seaters:

  • 40 chairs at ~₹2,500 each ≈ ₹1,00,000
  • 14 tables (mostly 2-seaters, ply-laminate tops, single/double pillar bases) at ~₹5,500–₹8,000 each ≈ ₹85,000–₹1,10,000
  • A few feature pieces (a marble-top table, bar stools, a booth) ≈ ₹40,000–₹80,000

That lands a 40-seat café in roughly the ₹2.5–₹4 lakh range for furniture — higher if you lean into premium tops and upholstery throughout.

Ready stock vs made-to-order

The other half of cost is time. Made-to-order furniture ties up your opening date in production lead times. Ready-stock pieces ship in days, so you can open on schedule — Gharnish carries 150+ chair designs and 25+ table varieties in stock, with 2 to 7 day pan-India delivery on in-stock items. For most new venues, a ready-stock core plus a few custom feature pieces is the best balance of cost, speed and character.

Common questions

How much does it cost to furnish a restaurant in India?

Complete dining sets run roughly ₹19,000–₹1,59,999. A 40-seat café typically lands around ₹2.5–₹4 lakh for furniture, more with premium materials throughout.

How much does a restaurant chair cost?

Commercial chairs start around ₹2,100 and rise with material, comfort and finish; bar stools and upholstered seating cost more.

How is a restaurant table priced?

Base plus top. Bases run from ~₹3,000 (single-pillar) to ₹12,000 (heavy wooden); tops are priced per square foot, ₹650 for ply-laminate up to ₹1,200 for Corian.

What's the cheapest way to furnish a café?

Ready-stock chairs and ply-laminate tops on economical pillar bases, leading with compact 2-seaters, and keeping premium materials for a few feature tables. Ready stock also avoids made-to-order cost and delay.
