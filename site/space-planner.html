About the Gharnish Space Planner

The Gharnish Space Planner is a free seating-capacity tool for restaurant, café and bar owners in India. Enter the dimensions of each room, choose your venue type, and the planner automatically works out how many tables fit, the best mix of 2, 4 and 6-seater tables, and your total guest count — shown on a simple top-down floor plan.

How to use the Space Planner

  1. Pick your venue type — café, fine-dine, bar or QSR — which sets sensible table sizes and spacing.
  2. Add your spaces — enter the length and width of each room or area in feet.
  3. Set the walking aisle — the clearance you want between tables for staff and guests.
  4. Build your plan — get an instant table layout, table mix and total seating capacity, then share it on WhatsApp for a quote.

Why use the Space Planner

  • Know your real seating capacity before you buy furniture.
  • Get the right table mix for your floor area and footfall.
  • Plan comfortable aisles and avoid overcrowding.
  • Move straight from layout to a furniture quote with ready-stock, pan-India delivery.

Gharnish is an import-led supplier of premium restaurant and café furniture, with 300+ restaurants and hotels furnished across India. Once you have planned your seating, use the Table Builder to price individual tables by base and top material.

Space Planner FAQ

Is the seating planner free? Yes — free to use with no sign-up.

How does it calculate seating capacity? From your room size, venue type and walking-aisle clearance it fits a mix of tables and totals the guest count.

Which venue types are supported? Café, fine-dine, bar and QSR, each with sensible table sizes and spacing.

Can I get furniture for my plan? Yes — share your plan on WhatsApp for a quote, with ready-stock, pan-India delivery.
