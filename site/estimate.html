Frequently asked questions

How much does restaurant furniture cost in India?

It ranges from economical to premium depending on materials and design — economy chairs start lower, while premium imported chairs, marble or sintered-stone tables and upholstered booths cost more. The calculator adds up your chairs, tables, stools and booths for an instant budget range, and you can confirm exact pricing on WhatsApp.

Is the estimate calculator free to use?

Yes, completely free. Add the quantities you need and get an instant furniture budget estimate — no sign-up required.

How accurate is the furniture estimate?

It gives a realistic budget range based on current Gharnish catalogue prices. Final pricing depends on the exact designs, materials and finishes you choose and any customisation — share your estimate on WhatsApp for a firm, itemised quote.

Do you deliver across India?

Yes. Gharnish holds a large ready-stock catalogue and delivers pan-India, usually within 2–7 working days, with hubs in Hyderabad and Bangalore. Made-to-order pieces have a stated lead time.

Can I get a custom quote for my restaurant?

Absolutely. After building your estimate, tap the WhatsApp button to send it to our team. We furnish restaurants, cafés, hotels and bars across India and will help finalise designs, sizes and a firm quote.
