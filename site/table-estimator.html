About the Gharnish Table Builder

The Gharnish Table Builder is a free online tool for restaurant, café, bar and hotel owners to design a custom commercial dining table and see live prices in seconds. Choose your venue type, pick a table base, and select a top material — the tool automatically sizes the table top and calculates transparent prices for 2-seater, 4-seater and 6-seater tables.

How to use the Table Builder

  1. Pick your venue — Café, QSR, Fine Dine or Bar / Lounge. This sets the correct top thickness and edge treatment for the way the table will be used.
  2. Choose a base — pillar, pedestal, cast-iron or heavy bases, or search our full Table Bases catalogue by name. Bases scale automatically for 2, 4 and 6 seaters.
  3. Select a top material — Ply Laminated, Marble, Sintered Stone, Terrazzo or Corian, each priced per square foot.
  4. Read your prices — instant 2 / 4 / 6-seater pricing with real dimensions. Download a branded PDF quotation or send an enquiry on WhatsApp.

Why use the Table Builder

  • Instant, transparent pricing — no waiting days for a manual quote.
  • Correct restaurant table sizes and seating for every venue type.
  • Mix any base with any top to match your interior style and budget.
  • Ready-stock velocity with fast pan-India delivery from Hyderabad and Bangalore.

Gharnish supplies premium imported restaurant and café furniture across India, with 300+ restaurants and hotels furnished. Use the Table Builder to plan a single table, or open the Space Planner to lay out a whole room and estimate seating capacity.

Table Builder FAQ

Is the table planner free? Yes — it is completely free with no sign-up.

Which table top materials can I choose? Ply Laminated, Marble, Sintered Stone, Terrazzo and Corian, each priced per square foot.

How are the 2, 4 and 6-seater prices calculated? Price = base + (top area × material rate) + edge, with the top size set automatically by your venue type.

Do you deliver across India? Yes — ready-stock items ship pan-India in 2–7 working days from Hyderabad and Bangalore.
